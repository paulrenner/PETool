Managing Cash Flows

What are Cash Flows?

Cash flows track money moving in and out of your investment:

  • Contributions: Capital calls - money you invest
  • Distributions: Returns - money you receive back

Adding Cash Flows

1 Click the gear icon (‚öô) next to an investment

Understanding Key Metrics

MOIC (Multiple on Invested Capital)

How many times your money has multiplied
Formula: (Distributions + NAV) √∑ Contributions

IRR (Internal Rate of Return)

Annualized rate of return on your investment
Accounts for timing of cash flows

Investment Return

Total profit/loss to date
Formula: (Distributions + NAV) - Contributions

Filtering & Searching

Filter Options

  • Filter by Group: View only investments in a specific group
  • Filter by Fund: View all accounts for a specific fund
  • Filter by Account: View a specific account
  • Search: Search by fund name, account number, or tags
  • Cutoff Date: Calculate metrics as of a specific date
Filters work together: Select a group, then a fund within that group, then an account - they cascade intelligently

Reset Filters: Click the ‚Üª icon to clear all filters
